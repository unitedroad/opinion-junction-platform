Our Motto

News Of The World aims to help its readers from all over the globe form a well-informed view of the current local and global issues.

We strongly believe that we can form a clearer and more balance picture only after we have heard the opposing views. Hence we feature the articles from in-house and guest writers belonging to broad social and political spheres.

While not having any official ideology of our own, helps us in our cause to equip the readers with means to make their own, well-informed opinions.

We also believe that the local and global news should be easily accessible for our readers.

And the local news for other regions should be a click away at times the readers need it.

Therefore, we have designed site interface keeping that in consideration. Please send your suggestions at contact@newsoftheworld.co to improve it further!

Company Profile

News of The World is a media company owned by the Fearless Voice Media group based in Delhi.

The contact information about News Of The World and Fearless Voice Media can be found at the Contact Us page.
